Random $p$-adic Matrices

12 January – 3 April 2026

We are reading texts on the theory of random $p$-adic matrices and Cohen–Lenstra heuristics. Our sources are

  • Topics in random matrix theory by Terence Tao
  • An Introduction to Random Matrices by Greg W. Anderson, Alice Guionnet, and Ofer Zeitouni
  • Probability theory for random groups arising in number theory by Melanie Matchett Wood
  • Heuristics on class groups of number fields by Henri Cohen and Hendrik W. Lenstra Jr.
  • Universality of cokernels of random matrix products by Hoi H. Nguyen and Roger Van Peski
  • Random integral matrices and the Cohen–Lenstra heuristics by Melanie Matchett Wood

We meet on Fridays at 10:00am. Contact Joshua Maglione if you are interested in joining.
